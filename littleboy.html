Little Boy — Radiation Zones

Initial ionizing radiation (gamma & neutron) from the 15-kiloton Little Boy uranium bomb, detonated at ~580 m altitude above Shima Hospital on 6 August 1945 (34.3946°N, 132.4543°E). Doses are free-in-air kerma for unshielded persons, based on DS02 dosimetry.
